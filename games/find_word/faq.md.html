**FIND WORD ALLL NITE FAQ** How do I load the game? : **Go to https://tracewords.com** on a phone, computer, console, etc. for the ideal full-screen experience. You can play however you like, but the game **plays best on a phone in portrait orientation** because selections are faster with a finger than a mouse or gamepad or keyboard. On iPhone, you can get back more screen area for the game by [hiding the Safari toolbar](https://support.apple.com/en-ca/guide/iphone/iphb3100d149/ios#:~:text=Change%20the%20display%20controls%20for,Safari%20app%20on%20your%20iPhone.&text=%2C%20then%20tap%20Hide%20Toolbar%20(tap,Show%20Reader%20(if%20available). If you have quadplay console, you can also run quadplay in kiosk mode and load FWAN from there, but it will be scaled to the quadplay emulator window. The game is part of the quadplay open source distribution. If you don't like that domain for some reason and trust itch.io or github more, then you can also play from https://shmuel797.itch.io/find-word or https://morgan3d.github.io/quadplay/games/find_word. How do I play? : Touch screen/mobile: drag your finger over letters to connect them in to a word. Repeat until the timer runs out. Press the curved arrow button on screen to rotate the board. Mouse: use the mouse to drag over letters. Press the curved arrow button on screen to rotate the board. Gamepad: use the D-pad or stick to move over a letter. Press the primary button (such as A or X) to select. Move the cursor and keep going. Press the secondary button (such as Y) to cancel. Use the right shoulder button to rotate. Keyboard: use WASD or the arrow keys to move the cursor. Use SPACE to select, ENTER or H to cancel, and RIGHT SHIFT or G to rotate the board. What is the scoring method? : The sum of the points of the letters, with a multiplier for length. See the table below. How do I earn XP and level up? : Play in Global mode. Your level, XP, and new abilities are saved after the leaderboard screen when the *_next_* match starts. Solo mode is for a calmer practice experience. What if the game server is offline or overloaded? : The server can't go down because there isn't a server. The app is fully distributed and peer-to-peer. Can I play in Global mode and earn XP if I am offline? : Yes, if you have already loaded the page and don't reload. Your stats and XP are stored on your phone or computer. If you reload then obviously you won't be able to load the webpage for the game. Can I play _with other people_ if I'm offline? : Yes. Everyone in the world sees the same board at the same time even if they are offline. So, you can play at the same time as someone else and discuss matches even without internet access. For example, two friends in a log cabin off grid can play next to each other on different phones and then compare scores. What's the deal with Moo and the other bots? : Keep leveling up to find out. Is the game done? How do I get the latest version? : New features are coming out all of the time. It's currently in open beta. Everything is backwards compatible, so your progress will be preserved when you hit reload on your browser to upgrade to the latest version. Expect new high level abilities and achievements, and narrative changes to be the main improvements going forward. Why isn't there an app? : Dealing with app stores sucks time and energy, and their licensing rules make it almost impossible for an open source game to exist. So I don't deal with that and just make the game free online. How do I move my progress to another computer or phone? : 1. Press the ⚙ gear button at the upper-left of the screen 2. Select "Export Save Game". It will make a `.savegame` file in Downloads or Files 3. Copy the file to the new computer or phone 4. On the new computer, press the ⚙ gear button and select "Import Save Game" Beta? What if it crashes? How do I back up my progress? : Export a save game as above and just keep it safe on your computer. Are the high scorers hacking? : Probably not. It is possible but slightly difficult to hack the game. Some people are really good. But as you play more you will both personally learn some strategies by looking at other people's words *and* you will unlock new in-game abilities that grant score and XP freees. But, but...what they did seems impossible! : High level players gain powers that you can only begin to imagine at lower levels. These powers are hard to use effectively, but players who have practiced and mastered them can do seemingly impossible things. I promise these aren't overpowered and it depends on skill, not grinding or abusing the powers. You'll get there, too. Why doesn't it show definitions? Or pronuciation? That would be educational! : Yes. I'm sorry we can't do that right now. I'm trying to source a high quality, reliable open source dictionary and work out how to make this function in game. For now, you definitely should take mystery words and look them up on your own! What about accessiblity? : The game supports touch screen, trackpad, mouse, keyboard, and various game controllers. It will automatically switch between those. Because of the nature of the game it is unfortunately impractical to add support for people with vision impairment. The game is designed to work well for different kinds of color vision and with hearing impairment. What about other languages than English? : English happens to work really well for word games because it has a ridiculous number of words (there are about 190k in this game's dictionary), a relatively small alphabet, and an incredibly liberal spelling grammar due to borrowing words from many sources. It is _possible_ to make word games in other languages. But, unfortunately, the gameplay is just not as interesting as in English.
